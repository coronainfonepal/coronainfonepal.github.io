Help Group - Nepal Fights Corona

Nepal Fights Corona - Help Group is an informal consortium of youths, activists, civil society organizations, experts of different fields formed in response to mitigating the risks of the global pandemic, COVID-19.

The main goal of the group is to be proved as an effective helping hand to Government and support them in any way possible in their fight against COVID-19.

{% include message-of-the-page.html msg="This group is a non-political and fully-volunteered driven group." class="notice" %}

Overview

Amid the rising panic due to the COVID-19 pandemic, a blistering concern over its effect in Nepal produced an urge to help the government among few people. With many calls among each other, they finally decided to form a group to better coordinate and execute all efforts in an effective manner. In this way, the group called "Nepal Fights Corona - Help Group" formed in social media.

Group Initiatives

NEWS Mediator

Provide a platform on Facebook to present as much as reliable news or information to the group members. There have been few guidelines set for any of the posts to be approved.
The goal here is to make people aware with reliable information, become the mediator to specific queries between people and concerned people.

View Post Guidelines
Medical Equipment Distribution

This group is also distributing medical equipments like PPE, Masks and others to the most needy one whether hospitals or individual ones. These equipments are procured by the effort of individuals or other helping groups.

Initiative Leads: Aanand Mishra

Project Ventilator

A group of engineers are working on building in-house ventilator which seems to be most required equipment in the coming days as the pandemic progresses. Nepal has shortage of ventilators even in good hospitals. With the intent that even if the working in-house ventilator is there, it might help to a certain level.

Initiative Leads: Sushant Dahal

Pregnancy Help
